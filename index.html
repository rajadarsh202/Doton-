🚨 CALL 112 IMMEDIATELY

Have your exact address ready. Stay calm and speak clearly.

112
DO
  • • Stay with the injured person
  • • Keep them calm and warm
  • • Apply pressure to bleeding wounds
  • • Follow dispatcher instructions exactly
  • • Check for breathing & pulse
DON'T
  • • Move someone with spinal injury
  • • Remove embedded objects from wounds
  • • Give food or water to unconscious person
  • • Attempt untrained procedures
  • • Panic or delay calling for help

⚡ First Aid Quick Reference
Heart Attack
Call 112. Keep them still. Loosen clothing. Give aspirin if not allergic.
Choking
5 back blows, 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich). Call 112 if not cleared.
Severe Bleeding
Apply firm pressure with clean cloth. Don't remove. Elevate if possible.
Stroke (F.A.S.T)
Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call 112.
